Displays

Class for hide content:

.hide: hide on all screen
.hide-mb-only: hide on mobile screen only
.hide-ts-down: hide on tablet small screen or below
.hide-tl-down: hide on tablet large screen or below
.hide-ds-down: hide on desktop small screen or below
.hide-dl-only: hide on desktop large screen only
.hide-ds-up: hide on desktop small screen or above
.hide-tl-up: hide on tablet large screen or above
.hide-ts-up: hide on tablet small screen or above
